Our History

Roasted Dreams opened in 1982 as a small family-owned café in Portland, offering hand-roasted coffee and homemade pastries daily

In the 1990s, it expanded, adding a cozy bookstore section, becoming a beloved spot for local artists and writers.

Today, Roasted Dreams is a thriving chain known for its unique blends, live music nights, and strong community ties nationwide.
